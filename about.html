Cayla Thompson is from Tyler Texas. She has recently graduated from the University of North Texas in December of 2017 with a 3.5 gpa. She acquired a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Science with a concentration in digital content and information systems. Orignially an art major, she has taken several fine art classes as well as digital art classes that add to her value as a graphic designer.

Since graduating, she has been acquiring online certificates in Adobe Illustrator, inDesign, and Photoshop. Not only does she have a passion for graphic design, but web development is also a strong interest. She is currently skilled in HTML, and CSS and is continuing to learn advanced web development techniques such as JavaScript, and Bootstrap.
